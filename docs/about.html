About SIS

SIS (Service Information System) is a new evaluation platform for agencies serving immigrants and refugees. It was developed by LogicalOutcomes, a Canadian nonprofit, in partnership with OCASI (the Ontario Council of Agencies Serving Immigrants), with funding from the Ontario Ministry of Citizenship and Immigration.

SIS lowers the cost and improves the value of monitoring and evaluation for nonprofits. Our web-based platform includes a complete set of evaluation tools, so it's easy to run evaluation projects. From creating an evaluation plan, to collecting and managing data, to generating attractive and insightful reports - SIS provides agencies the tools they need to measure program outcomes and communicate results.

What's so great about SIS?

1. State-of-the-art evaluation design

We start with a tailor-made logic model to guide you through your evaluation.
Our high-quality indicators measure program outcomes the right way. This means that findings can be shared, compared, and aggregated across the sector.

2. Trouble-free data collection

We offer survey templates with pre-populated questions to help you track client demographics, health, education, social inclusion, finances and more.
We provide many options for data collection. Staff and clients can respond to online surveys on a computer or a mobile phone. You can conduct telephone interviews or print surveys for manual data entry.

3. Secure data storage

Data is stored on Canadian servers and protected by multiple layers of encryption and authentication, so responses from staff and clients remain private and confidential.

4. Powerful analysis and reporting

DOur powerful data visualization and reporting engine makes it easy to analyze program impact, view findings, and generate reports.
Our interactive data portal uses information from Statistics Canada to create charts and graphs showing income and employment trends for immigrants in your community. Use it to establish need in your community or add context to your reports.

The Partners

OCASI was formed in 1978 to act as a collective voice for immigrant-serving agencies and to coordinate responses to shared needs and concerns. OCASI is a registered charity governed by a volunteer board of directors. Its membership is comprised of more than 225 community-based organizations in Ontario.

LogicalOutcomes is a Canadian nonprofit with a mission to build organizational evaluation and learning capacity to improve lives. Over the past two years we have successfully implemented ten evaluation systems built on District Health Information Software (DHIS2), the software platform that forms the basis for SIS.ngo.
